All RIFE2 Examples

All the examples are included in this site. These are their URLs:

HelloAuthentication

  • Open your browser at {{v route:authentication.landing/}}

HelloContentManagement

  • Then add a news item by going to {{v route:cmf.add/}}
  • Delete the database through h{{v route:cmf.remove/}}
